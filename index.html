 WHAT ARE STORAGE DEVICES?


   A storage device is a piece of computer hardware that is used to store information.
   electronically. Some examples of storage devices include, HDDs(Hard Drives),
   SSDs(Solid State Drives), Flash Drives, MicroSD cards, etc.

   Here we will be focusing on two of the most commonly used devices, Hard drives,
   and Solid State Drives. We will be providing an insight on their general usages
   and histories. Additionally, we will also be comparing the two to explain which
   device is better in the long run.
